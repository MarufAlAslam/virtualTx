Who we are

VirtualTX was created by the same owners as RemoteSpeech and Abington Speech Pathology Services, a multi-faceted rehabilitation company that has provided in-person and remote staffing and support throughout the country for more than 20 years Our leadership team I comprised of working clinicians who know what is needed to meet the needs of clients in a changing landscape. We partnered with an innovative tech team to create what we know SLPs and other therapists really want and need!

Our therapists enjoy a library of therapy materials while also being able to take care of the business needs that are required of busy therapists - video capability, scheduling, notes, billing and more! With advanced state-of-the-art games, activities, and interactive screen sharing, students and adults will actually look forward to their therapy sessions. VirtualTx has an unlimited potential to transform therapy as we know it!
