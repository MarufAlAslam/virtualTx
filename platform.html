VirtualTx
Platform

VirtualTx is a complete Ai-driven platform, tailored for a seamless teletherapy experience. VirtualTx is a multi-user friendly interface with includes all of the features for ideal teletherapy: scheduling, live chat, a materials library, billing, online assessments, and cloud storage for session notes and reports capabilities. The platform can be customized to meet the needs of all therapists and their clients. SLPs, occupational therapists and other educational specialists will enjoy the unlimited potential of VirtualTx!

01

A Vast Collection Of Therapy Links & Material

An all advanced and technologically driven customisable and expert picked comprehensive links and material library to enhance the quality of our therapy solutions.

02

Interactive Gaming

An Intelligent platform providing ultimate therapy-gaming experience with Real-Time Gaming and UI, along with AR & VR, resulting in instant solutions and supporting greater client engagement.

03

Dedicated Options To Enter Notes, Goals and Evaluations

Make notes with us, evaluate your time and meetings, set goals for yourselves and/or your clients.

04

Embedded Electronic Client Forms

An all embedded electronic client forms to publish your customised forms.

05

Direct client/therapist chat and homework share

Instant messaging chat box for direct interaction between clients and therapist for enhanced and influential impact also to share homework and notes.

06

Direct Online Client Invoicing And Payment.

Easy billing and direct online client invoicing with us for any and all services opted. Our integrated billing and all in one invoicing solutions keeps the right pace of your time & business.

07

Daily Ledger View

A complete ledger view to keep a track of your daily activities and events.

08

Goal/Progress Charting

Automated charting to keep you updated with your goals and progress.

09

Real-time Feedback On Client Engagement & Performance

Instant feedback with real-time client engagement and performance status updates.
